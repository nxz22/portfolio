Overview

In this project, I pushed the boundaries of gaming, artificial intelligence, and animal interaction by creating a program that allows real-life monkeys to engage in a game of Pokémon Emerald. By modifying the game to include a monkey character and developing a machine learning model to interpret the monkey's movements, I created a unique experience that I humored myself using.

Context

Monkeys playing video games would be funny.

The inspiration for this project stemmed from a desire to explore the potential of integrating animals with video games in a humorous way. The challenge was to create a system that could accurately interpret the monkey's movements and translate them into in-game actions. This required a comprehensive understanding of both game modding and machine learning.
